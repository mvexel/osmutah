About OpenStreetMap Utah

Established in 2011, we are a group of GIS and mapping enthusiasts from the Salt Lake City area focused on improving OpenStreetMap through monthly meetups and collaborative projects.

We hold monthly Map Nights open to the public — everyone is welcome regardless of experience level.
