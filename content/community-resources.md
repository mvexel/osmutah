## Mapillary Camera Loan Program

We have a **GoPro MAX 360-degree camera kit** available for community members to borrow. The kit includes:

- GoPro MAX camera
- 128GB SD card
- Selfie stick

The camera is available at Map Night events on an informal loan basis. Just ask!

This program is modeled after the [OpenStreetMap U.S. Camera Grant program](https://openstreetmap.us) and is designed to help capture street-level imagery for OpenStreetMap using [Mapillary](https://www.mapillary.com/).
