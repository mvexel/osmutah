OpenStreetMap Utah is one of the oldest local OpenStreetMap communities in the United States, operating since 2011.

## What We Do

- Organize monthly mapping events open to the public
- Engage in collaborations and partnerships with local and state GIS agencies
- Work with broader U.S. OpenStreetMap communities

## Support OpenStreetMap

OpenStreetMap is a mostly volunteer-run organization that relies on donations to keep going.

There are two ways to support our work:

1. **Donate to OpenStreetMap U.S.** — Your donation helps support the broader OpenStreetMap ecosystem in the United States.
2. **Get in touch** — If you'd like to discuss sponsorship opportunities specific to our local community, [contact us](/contact/).
