We hold monthly **Map Nights** on the second Tuesday of each month. All experience levels are welcome!

## Mall Mapping Sessions

Mall Mapping is a great winter indoor mapping activity. We've been mapping Utah's malls and shopping centers to improve OpenStreetMap data. Completed locations include various malls across the Wasatch Front.

Future sites: Jordan Landing, Station Park, The Shops at South Town, City Creek.

## Park City Excursion

We're planning a spring mapping trip to Park City's historic Main Street.
