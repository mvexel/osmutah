## Map Nights

The best way to connect with us is to [join us at a Map Night](/our-map-nights/). We meet monthly and welcome everyone regardless of experience level.

Join the `#local-utah` channel on the OpenStreetMap US Slack. [Sign up for Slack here](http://slack.openstreetmap.us).
