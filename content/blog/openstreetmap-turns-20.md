OpenStreetMap turns **20 years old** on August 9! To celebrate, we're hosting a special event.

## The Details

**When:** August 9 (OSM's birthday!)
**Where:** [The Local](https://thelocalsaltlakecity.com) — a food hall near Library TRAX station in Salt Lake City
**What:** Happy hour and good company. Food at your own expense.

Come celebrate two decades of free, open map data with fellow mappers and enthusiasts.

Learn more about the [OSM 20th Birthday celebrations worldwide](https://birthday20.openstreetmap.org).
