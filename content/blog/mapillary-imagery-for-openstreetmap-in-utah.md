[Mapillary](https://www.mapillary.com/) is a crowdsourced street-level imagery platform — think Google Street View, but open-licensed and community-driven. I've been contributing to Mapillary for over a decade.

## Getting Started

If you're new to Mapillary, check out the [getting started guide](https://blog.mapillary.com/update/2022/08/31/getting-started-with-mapillary.html). You can capture imagery with just your phone!

## Coverage in Utah

Utah has moderate Mapillary coverage. Salt Lake City has substantial imagery, but recent coverage (less than 2 years old) is thinner than we'd like.

## Our Camera Lending Program

OSM Utah has a **GoPro MAX** 360-degree camera available for community members to borrow. This captures much higher quality imagery than a phone and covers every direction at once.

We held our inaugural capturing session on August 21 at Woodbine Food Hall, where members could learn how to use the camera and start capturing imagery in their neighborhoods.

Want to borrow the camera? Come to a [Map Night](/our-map-nights/) and ask!
