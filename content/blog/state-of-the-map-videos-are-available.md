Great news! The recordings from the 2024 **State of the Map** conference in Salt Lake City are now available online.

The conference was a fantastic event, and we received positive feedback about the quality of local OpenStreetMap data in the Salt Lake City area. It's great to see our community's work recognized.

If you missed any sessions or want to revisit your favorites, head over and check out the videos.
