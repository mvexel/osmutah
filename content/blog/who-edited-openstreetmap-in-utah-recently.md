Want to know who's been editing OpenStreetMap in your area? There's a great tool for that.

## Latest OpenStreetMap Edits per Tile

The [Latest OSM Edits per Tile](https://resultmaps.neis-one.org/osm-edits-tile/#13/40.7482/-111.8733) tool shows a grid of blue cells representing recent edits. The darker the blue, the more recent the activity.

### Features

- **Search** — Find any location
- **Time filters** — View edits from the last 1, 3, 7, or 14 days
- **Mapper profiles** — Click a cell to see who edited
- **Tag key filtering** — Filter by specific tag keys
- **Saved filter combinations** — Save your favorite views

### Pro Tip

You can subscribe to **RSS feeds** for edit notifications in specific areas. This is a great way to stay on top of what's happening in your neighborhood or area of interest.
