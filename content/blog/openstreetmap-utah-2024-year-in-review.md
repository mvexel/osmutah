What a year 2024 was for OpenStreetMap in Utah! Here's a look at the numbers.

## Pedestrian Infrastructure

- 11,246 km of new sidewalks
- 4,884 km enhanced
- 13,738 curbs added

## Roads

- 3,115 km added
- 3,186 km improved

## Buildings

- 67,998 new buildings
- 6,139 enhanced

## Points of Interest

- 412 new restaurants (1,534 updates)
- 169 new shops (307 updated)
- 122 new cultural facilities

## Outdoor

- 800 km of new hiking trails
- 411 km enhanced
- 34,959 natural features added

## Tools We Used

- [Rapid editor](https://rapideditor.org/) for AI-assisted editing
- [Mapillary](https://www.mapillary.com/) for street-level imagery
- Shared 360-degree camera from our lending program

## Community Highlights

We participated in the [Trails Stewardship Initiative](https://openstreetmap.us/our-work/trails/) and attended [State of the Map US 2024](https://openstreetmap.us/events/state-of-the-map-us/2024/) right here in Salt Lake City.

Our monthly Map Nights continued at [Woodbine Food Hall](https://www.woodbineslc.com/) and we welcomed many new mappers to the community.
