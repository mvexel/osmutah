As 2024 comes to a close, we're reflecting on what has been our strongest year in 13 years of OpenStreetMap Utah.

## Highlights

- Monthly **Map Nights** at Woodbine Food Hall brought together mappers old and new
- Our **GoPro Max 360 camera lending program** for Mapillary imagery capture took off
- **State of the Map US** brought 250+ attendees to the University of Utah
- The **Trails Stewardship Initiative** made significant progress on Utah trail data

## By the Numbers

- 3,500+ new mapper edit sessions (up from 2,000 the previous year)
- 32,000 total edit sessions
- 1,700 MapRoulette sessions
- ~3,000 POI sessions
- 13,000+ infrastructure sessions
- ~4,700 natural feature sessions

Check out the [Utah Trail Campaign](https://tasks.openstreetmap.us/explore?campaign=Utah+Trail+Campaign+%28TSI%29&omitMapResults=1) on the Tasking Manager.

Happy holidays from all of us at OpenStreetMap Utah!
