Join us at Woodbine Food Hall on Wednesday, July 10 at 6pm. We meet as usual at [Woodbine Food Hall](https://www.woodbineslc.com/), located at 545 W 700 S in Salt Lake City. 

We will map together, share the latest from the OSM world and enjoy the company of fellow mappers. 

Never mapped before? Just learning about OpenStreetMap? You're especially welcome! We're excited to have new people of all ages and backgrounds join the mapping community.
