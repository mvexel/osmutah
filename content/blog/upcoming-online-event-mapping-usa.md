[Mapping USA](https://openstreetmap.us/events/mapping-usa/2025/) is a free online event organized by OpenStreetMap U.S. It features talks, panels, workshops, and collaborative mapping sessions.

The event covers exciting projects like SliceOSM and Americana, and includes office hours for popular tools like OSMCha, MapRoulette, and OpenHistoricalMap.

Whether you're a seasoned mapper or just getting started, there's something for everyone.
