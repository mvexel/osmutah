Mall Mapping is a great winter surveying activity. At our last Map Night, we discussed planning a mall mapping session. We analyzed data showing when shops and restaurants in various Utah malls were last edited in OpenStreetMap, using color-coded visualizations to identify which locations need the most attention.

## Malls We're Looking At

**City Creek** (Salt Lake City) — The famously-closed-on-Sundays downtown mall.

**Fashion Place Mall** — Home to the only Crate & Barrel and Container Store in Utah.

**Shops at South Town** — Mapped in July 2024 by leftybender.

**Valley Fair** — Anchored by a bowling center.

**Jordan Landing** — On our future mapping list.

**Shops at Fort Union** — Another target for mapping sessions.

**The Gateway** — Open-air mall in downtown SLC.

**Station Park** (Farmington) — Planned for a future session.

Want to help? Discuss on `#local-utah` in the [OSM US Slack](http://slack.openstreetmap.us).
