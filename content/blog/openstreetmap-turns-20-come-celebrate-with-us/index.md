It's hard to believe that it's been 20 years! [OpenStreetMap turns 20](https://birthday20.openstreetmap.org) on August 9, and we are celebrating! For this occasion, we're trying a different venue, [The Local](https://thelocalsaltlakecity.com), another food hall that opened recently. It's right by the Library TRAX station. Join us there on Friday, August 9 for a happy hour drink and a bite to eat! (At your own expense, until we find a sponsor..)

Check our [Events page](https://new.osmutah.org/our-map-nights/) to learn more and RSVP!
